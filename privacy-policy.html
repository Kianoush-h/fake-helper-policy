Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Call Me ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Call Me does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter in the app (such as contact names, phone numbers, and message content) is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google's Advertising Policies.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications — to deliver scheduled fake calls, texts, and vibration alerts
  • Vibration — to simulate incoming call vibrations
  • Audio — to play ringtone and text tone sounds

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and not for data collection.

Data Storage

All data remains on your device. We do not operate any servers or databases. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
